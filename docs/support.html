Frequently asked

Does the app need internet access?
No. Everything runs and is stored locally on your device.

Is my data backed up if I get a new phone?
Data is stored in local app storage on-device only, so it does not transfer automatically. There is currently no export/backup feature.

How do I request a bar be added or corrected?
Email the address above with the bar name and neighborhood.
