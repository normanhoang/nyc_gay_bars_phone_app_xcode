Privacy Policy

NYC Gay Bars · Last updated June 27, 2026

NYC Gay Bars (“the app”) is built so that your data stays on your device. We do not operate a server, and the app makes no network requests at runtime.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on any server. It has no accounts, no sign-up, and no analytics.

Data stored on your device

Your check-ins, logged drinks, history, and earned badges are saved only in local storage on your device. This information never leaves your phone and is removed if you delete the app.

Location

If you grant location permission, the app uses your location only on your device to show your distance to bars and sort them by proximity. Your location is never transmitted off the device, never stored, and never shared. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings; the app still works without it.

Links to other apps

The app may open Instagram (via the Instagram app or website) when you tap a bar’s social link. Once you leave the app, the privacy policy of that service applies. We have no control over and take no responsibility for third-party services.

Children

The app is intended for adults of legal drinking age and is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
